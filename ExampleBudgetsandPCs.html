Example Budgets for PC's

    $400 PC Budget - $400

    This Build is for beginners or for people not really commiting to their PC. It is good for the experience but the PC won't be as functional and powerful as other higher Budget PCs.





    $500 PC Budget - $500

    This build is also known as "The Console Killer" and is a really good value PC. This is an entry level PC, but it provides great value and experience





    $750 PC Budget - $750"

    This PC budget allows for great experience for building while allowing an amazing experience on PC. This is really good for how much you are paying. This budget is usually the most popular.





    $1000 PC Budget - $1000

    This is the best building experience without getting too hard. The performance is above average.





    $1500 PC Budget - $1500

    This budget is when you want something better than what you will find for the price. If you are switching from prebuilt, this is the most popular choice.





    $2000 PC Budget - $2000

    This Budget and above is for real enthusiasts genuinely interested in PC building. This is one of the most popular choices for a seriously powerful system.





    No Budget - Click If You Have Too Much Money

    This Budget is if you are dead serious about building a PC. This pc budget is generally for those who have built before and those who know exactly what they want in their system. This PC setup is most likely used by gamers and streamers or other intensive worktasks.
